Abel Cerda

CEO & Founder

Growing up I always found myself lost in thought wondering how far humanity could go if they just had proper tools that could help them continuously develop and adjust to the unfairness of the universe. I then made it my mission in life to seek the limits of technology. In order to reach such a goal, I dedicated countless hours towards creating this company, I found others like me who are willing to work towards the same goal. We as a whole, made it our mission to continuously develop new hardware and software that could assist others in their day to day life. As technology becomes widely accessible, our hardware will continue to be supplied.
